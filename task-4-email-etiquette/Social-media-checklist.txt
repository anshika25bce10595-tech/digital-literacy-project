SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST
* SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS

* Do’s:
* Use strong and secure passwords.
* Be respectful while posting or commenting.
* Verify information before sharing.
* Keep your profile privacy settings secure.
* Think before posting anything online.

* Don’ts:
* Do not share personal information publicly.
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
* Do not engage in cyberbullying.
* Do not share OTP or passwords.
* Do not post inappropriate content.
