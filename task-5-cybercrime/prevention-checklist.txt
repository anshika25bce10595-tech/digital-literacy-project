PREVENTION CHECKLIST
STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST


1) Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
2) Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
3) Always verify payment requests before accepting.
4) Use trusted and secure apps for online transactions.
5) Enable two-factor authentication for accounts.
6) Regularly check your bank account statements.
7) Do not respond to calls asking for personal or banking details.
8) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.

Reporting:
If you face any cybercrime, report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number 1930.
